ILMKIDUNIYA


Ilmkiduniya is Pakistan's leading educational website with more than 30 different informative sections. It is the best website for all sorts of study related problems for university and college students.


HAMARIWEB


Hamariweb is the Pakistan's first bi-lingual website for all age groups ie. students, professionals, youth, parents etc. Hamariweb provides all the daily essentials under one roof.


EDUVISION


Eduvision is a Pakistani website htat provides the largest non-profit career counseling and educational guidance. It has been providing educational opportunities nationwide since 2001.


KITAAB


Kitaab is one of the biggest and most reliable online website for all sort of news, whether it is the latest exam result, upcoming exams, admissions, updated merit lists or news on armed forces


GOTEST


Gotest is Pakistan's first online website which allows high school and college students, to prepare and check their preparation status subjectwise via online tests. Gotest is the first website where all classes for all provincial boards are taught. It also prepares students for CSS Exams Test, Armed Forced Test, NTS, NAT, GAT Test.


SABAQ


Sabaq creates world class tutoring videos for both english and urdu medium students, free of cost. It prepares students of all boards of Pakistan. Subjects include Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology, English, General Science, Computer Science, General Math, Statistics and Accounting. It also has tutoring videos for kindergarden level and prepares students for MCAT and ECAT.


W3SCHOOLS


W3schools is a free web developers resource. It has been focusing on simple and easy learning since 1998. W3Schools' tutorials start from basic level and move all the way up to professional references.


KHAN ACADEMY


Khan academy is a learning tool for all age groups, parents and teachers included. Khan academy provides pratice exercises and instructions. It allows learners to learn at their own pace inside and outside the classroom. It tackles math, science, computing, history, art history, economics, and more, including K-14 and test preparation (SAT, Praxis, LSAT) content.
