DAWN NEWS


Dawn news is Pakistan's oldest english language newspaper. It also runs a 24-hour news channels. It provides the fastest and most authentic news. It provides national and international news. It also has sections for Multimedia, Technology, Sports, Culture, Business etc.


THE NEWYORK TIMES


The New York Times is an American daily newspaper based in New York City with a worldwide influence and readership. The NewYork Times oncludes news regarding Business, Sports, Arts, Science, Styles, Home, Travel, and other features.


BBC NEWS


The British Broadcasting Corporation (BBC) is a public service broadcaster. It is the world's oldest national broadcaster. BBC includes sections: NEWS, Weather, Sports, Children's, Entertainment, Religon and Ethics, Music, Learning, Factual, Drama and Comedy.


JANG NEWS


Jang news is the oldest Urdu-language newspaper. It also runs a number of television channels under GEO television networks. Jang news offers a combination of main news and special interest sections. Throughout the week, the main pages offer national and international news, business and finance, comment and analysis and daily sports coverage.


BOL NEWS


Bol news comes under the Bol networks which is media conglomerate. Bol news cover almost every aspect of our lives. It informs its viewers and readers with the latest national and international news. Not only that, it also covers Entertainment, Sports and Health sections.


ESPN


ESPN is an American multinational basic cable sports channel. It is one of the most successful sports networks. ESPN broadcasts in more than 200 countries.
