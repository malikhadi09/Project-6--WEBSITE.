DISCORD


Discord is an instant messaging and digital distribution platform designed to create communities. Discord is the easiest way to talk over voice, video, and text. It also allows you to chat, hang out, and stay connected with your friends and communities.


FACEBOOK


Facebook an online social media and social networking service. You can create a profile consensually reveal information about yourself. You can post text, photos and multimedia which is shared with any other users that have agreed to be your "friend", or, with a different privacy setting, with any reader. You can also use various embedded apps, join common-interest groups, buy and sell items or services on Marketplace, and receive notifications about the recent activities of your friends and pages you follow.


INSTAGRAM


Instagram is a photo and video sharing social networking service. The app allows you to upload media that can be edited with filters and organized by hashtags and geographical tagging. Posts can be shared publicly or with pre-approved followers. You can browse the content of other users by tags and locations and view trending content. You can also like photos and follow other users.


TWITTER


Twitter is a microblogging and social networking service on which user can post and interact through messages known as "tweets". You can only post, like and retweet tweets if you are registered, if you are not registered then you can only read them.
